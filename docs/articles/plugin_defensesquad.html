BasicDefenseSquad

Use case

A plugin for defending a location against threats.
The group will split to two sub-groups:

  • Extreme danger detail: this group of pirates will be used to defend against imminent (imidiate) threats.
  • Regular danger detail: this group of pirates will attempt eliminating possible (& further) possible threats (for example an enemy boosted pirate) in addition to optimization of our position.

Usage

  • Constructor #1
Field Description
Camp The location to defend
ExtremeDangerDetailCount Amount of pirates to reserve for the Extreme Danger Detail
PossibleDangers A function that returns all possible threats/dangers
ExtremeDangerFunction A mapping between a possible threat and its extreme defense stats
DangerFunction A mapping between a possible threat and its extreme defense stats
Note

A defense stats is an object representing how to defend against a specific threat. It contains a tuple (rank, amount). rank is the threat rank (severity) amount is the amount of pirates required to counter-act it
